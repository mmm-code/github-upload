
Course Title: How to Create Business Plan in One Hour

Target Student
What knowledge & tools are required?
* Have an idea for a business.
* Want to get the idea on paper and put it to action 
      quickly.
* Be able to perform internet research for your chosen 
      business industry.	
	
Who should take this course?
* Want to understand the main parts of a business plan.
* Want to focus on the core of your business and create 
      a business strategy.
* Avoid confusion and frustration from misleading 
      information about writing a business plan.
* You want to share your business plan with like 
      minded people and receive feedback on your ideas.
* This course is not for someone wanting to write a business model.
* This course is not for someone who does not believe that 
      writing a business plan will help them create a business.	
* This course is not for someone who already knows how to 
      write business plans.
	
	

What will students achieve or be able to do after taking your course?
* At the end of the course students will be able to
Quickly and easily place your ideas for a business on paper.
* At the end of the course students will be able to
Use a business plan template to fill in the blanks to your business plan.
* At the end of the course students will be able to
Create a business using the business plan your created in this course 
      as a guide.	
* At the end of the course students will be able to
      differentiate every aspect of your business.
* At the end of the course students will be able to
Answer fundamental questions that pertain to your unique business.
* At the end of the course students will be able to
    explain who their target customer is for their business.
* At the end of the course students will be able to
create competitive strengths and weaknesses of your business 
      in your given market.
* At the end of the course students will be able to
demonstrate their value proposition to their customer.	
* At the end of the course students will be able to
 how to refine your business idea to create a successful business.
* At the end of the course students will be able to
 if you have a good idea for a business.
